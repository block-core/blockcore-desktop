Blockcore Hub does not currently collect any information about you as a person. This can change in the future, with new features available. A change to the privacy policy, will be announced.

Whenever you perform transactions, the transactions is store on a public chain of blocks (blockchain). You can never remove this information, they are locked in the chain.

Full mode is the only mode that gives you full privacy. This ensures your instance of Blockcore Hub is downloading the full blockchain for you to validate, and for you to verify your own transactions on. Nobody can discover what addresses you own, if you use the full-mode. Other modes can potentially leak your wallet address to third parties.
